1. Fill KYC Details

Operators securely log in and complete guided KYC forms: personal details, banking information, employment history and branch assignment. Inline validation helps prevent common errors (Aadhaar/PAN formats, required fields), and all data is transmitted over TLS and stored encrypted for compliance.

2. Upload Documents

Upload Aadhaar, PAN, bank proof and other required documents as images or PDFs. The uploader supports camera capture on mobile, automatic file-type validation, recommended size limits, and optional OCR-assisted extraction to pre-fill form fields for faster submission.

3. Review & Verify

Employees review submissions, approve or request resubmission with annotated feedback, and record the verification outcome. Admins have a consolidated view for oversight, audit logs, escalations and exportable reports to support compliance and dispute resolution.
